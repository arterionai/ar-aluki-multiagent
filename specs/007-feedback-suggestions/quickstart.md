# Quickstart: Feedback & Suggestions

## What is a Suggestion?

A suggestion is feedback about the Aluki platform itself—feature requests, bug reports, improvements, or other product ideas. Suggestions are separate from your personal memories and help Arterion improve the product.

## How to Submit a Suggestion

**In chat**:
```
"I have a suggestion: add dark mode"
"Bug: reminders don't show up on my calendar"
"Feature request: remind me at different times for different zones"
```

## Attachments

You can include:
- Text descriptions
- Screenshots or photos
- Audio/voice notes for context

All attachments are stored securely and reviewed by the Arterion team.

## Rewards & Incentives

- **Submission bonus**: Earn credits for submitting a suggestion
- **Quality bonus**: Additional rewards if your suggestion is implemented
- **Status updates**: Get notified when your suggestion is reviewed or shipped

## Privacy

- Suggestions are reviewed by internal Arterion staff only
- Your feedback is never shared publicly without permission
- You can view the status of your suggestions anytime

## Related Features

- **Admin Dashboard** (008a): Arterion team reviews and classifies suggestions
- **Personal Memory** (002): Capture notes and ideas separately from suggestions
