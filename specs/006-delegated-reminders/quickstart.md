# Quickstart: Delegated Reminders

## What is a Delegated Reminder?

A delegated reminder is when you ask the system to remind someone else on your behalf. This is useful for:
- Reminding teammates of deadlines
- Following up with external contacts
- Distributing tasks among team members

## How to Send a Delegated Reminder

**In chat**:
```
"Remind John to send me the proposal by Friday"
"Tell Alice to review the design changes on Monday"
"Remind the team about the meeting tomorrow at 10 AM"
```

## Prerequisites

Before you can send a delegated reminder:
1. The recipient must have **granted you consent** to send reminders on their behalf
2. You can view their consent status or request it if not granted

## How It Works

1. You specify the reminder recipient and details
2. System checks consent (blocks if not granted)
3. At the scheduled time, recipient receives notification
4. Delivery attempts are logged and reported back to you

## Limits & Quotas

- Max 10 delegated reminders per recipient per 24 hours
- Requires active consent from recipient
- Failed deliveries do not count against the limit
